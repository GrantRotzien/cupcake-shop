Martha Stewart and Snoop Dogg's Special Cupcakes

Preheat oven to 350° F. Grease muffin pan or add paper muffin liners. Recipe makes 12-16 cupcakes. In a large bowl combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa powder and salt. Set dry mixture aside. In another large bowl cream together the cannabis butter or oil and the sugar. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating the mixture until well combined. Stir in the vanilla. Add the dry mixture and the milk alternately. Beat well until mixture has a uniform consistency. Pour mixture into muffin cups filling each one 3/4 full. Bake at 350°F for 15-18 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Allow to cool for 10-15 minutes then add your favorite frosting.

  • 4 tbsp cannabis butter or oil
  • 1 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 lrg eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
